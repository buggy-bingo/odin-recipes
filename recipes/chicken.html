Chicken

Description

Chicken breast is a lean, mild-flavored cut that grills quickly and stays juicy when cooked properly.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breasts
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Optional: garlic powder or herbs

Steps

  1. Preheat grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Brush chicken with olive oil and season with salt, pepper, and optional spices.
  3. Grill chicken 6-8 minutes per side until fully cooked.
  4. Let rest a few minutes before slicing and serving.
