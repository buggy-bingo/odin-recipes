Salmon

Description

Salmon steak is a rich, tender cut with a slightly oily texture that grills beautifully, developing a crisp exterior while staying moist inside.

Ingredients

  • Salmon steak
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Optional: lemon slices or dill

Steps

  1. Preheat grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Brush salmon with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Grill 4-5 minutes per side until cooked through.
  4. Serve with lemon slices or fresh dill if desired.
